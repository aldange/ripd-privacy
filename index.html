RIPD Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

RIPD is developed by Alex D'Angelillo.

Data Collection

RIPD collects no data about any users. There are no cookies or analytics. RIPD uses:

  • No personal information
  • No usage analytics
  • No cookies
  • No tracking
  • No third-party analytics
  • No login or account

Your Data

All workout tracking happens entirely on your device:

  • No data leaves your device
  • No data is uploaded to a server
  • No data is stored anywhere but your device
  • Data is only processed in your device's memory

You can delete the app and all its data at any time.

GDPR Compliance

We comply with GDPR by not collecting or processing any personal data. There is no need for cookie banners or consent forms. Therefore there is no data to access, correct, delete, or migrate.
